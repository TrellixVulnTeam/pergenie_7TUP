  4. What is VCF?

    VCF (Variant Call Format) is a typical data format for DNA sequencing data from NGS.

    Example:

    ##fileformat=VCFv4.1
...
#CHROM POS     ID        REF    ALT     QUAL FILTER INFO                FORMAT      NA00001
20     14370   rs6054257 G      A       29   PASS   NS=3;DP=14;AF=0.5   GT:GQ:DP:HQ 0|0:48:1:51,51
20     17330   .         T      A       3    q10    NS=3;DP=11;AF=0.017 GT:GQ:DP:HQ 0|0:49:3:58,50

  9. [Advanced] How do you check strands of SNPs?

    In GWAS Catalog, strands of risk_alleles are not provided. But we handle all the genotypes of users in plus strands in perGENIE. So we check strands of risk_alleles refering dbSNP. If strands of risk_alleles seem minus, we reverse it.
